About us

Who we are:

Ponta Grossa Tourism is a travel agency organization dedicated to promoting tourism to the city of Ponta Grossa, Brazil. Our mission is to showcase the city's diverse attractions, including its natural beauty, rich history, and vibrant culture. We believe that Ponta Grossa is a unique and special place, and we are committed to helping visitors have an unforgettable experience. We offer a variety of services to tourists, including information about the city's attractions, transportation, and accommodation. We also organize tours and events to help visitors discover the best of Ponta Grossa. We are passionate about our city, and we believe that tourism has the power to enrich the lives of both visitors and locals. We invite you to join us in exploring Ponta Grossa and all that it has to offer.
