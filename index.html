About us

Welcome to Ponta Grossa Tourism! Ponta Grossa is a major transportation hub and a city with a rich cultural heritage. It offers a variety of natural, historical, and cultural attractions, as well as a vibrant nightlife. Visitors can explore the towering sandstone formations of Vila Velha State Park, swim in the crystal-clear waters of Buraco do Padre cave, or hike to the Mariquinha Waterfall. The city's museums, such as the Campos Gerais Museum, house a wealth of information about the region's history and culture. In the evening, visitors can enjoy a delicious meal at one of Ponta Grossa's many restaurants, followed by a night out at a bar, club, or theater. There is something for everyone in Ponta Grossa, making it a great destination for a vacation or weekend getaway.
