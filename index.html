About

Hello! I am Larissa. I am looking for a job as a Junior Full Stack Web Developer to practice the skills I adquired during the Web Development Bootcamp I did at Ironhack - São Paulo. Those are: Javascript (ES6), MongoDB, Express, React, NodeJS, HTML & CSS. During the Bootcamp I developed 3 projects, each in 1 week (There are more details about them bellow). Now I am currently studying Python and I plan to learn #C next.

I am originally from Brazil but I currently live in Ireland. Here I had many jobs that tought me to be resilient and to work well in teams. Most of my employment history consists in Customer Service and Sales.

What attracted me to technology is its importance today. Technology directly influences the quality of life of a society, and therefore, people's well-being and mental health. Due to the possibility of improving lives, by solving problems and offering solutions in creative ways, technology made me desire to start a Web Development career.

As I am very motivated and passionate about Web Development. I believe, if I was given the opportunity, I would do a good job and be very useful to the company I'm in.
